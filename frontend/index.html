Our Mission

Food insecurity is one of Africa's most pressing challenges. According to the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO), nearly 37% of food produced in Sub-Saharan Africa is lost or wasted before it reaches consumers, while millions of people experience hunger.

ChopNow addresses this issue by leveraging digital technology to connect food vendors with surplus meals to consumers seeking affordable options. Together, we're building a sustainable solution that benefits everyone.
